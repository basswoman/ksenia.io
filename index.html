Sound Design Work

Microtonal Subtractive Synth KSSL #9 and it's software version with 3D user interface

My little synth made it to the semi-final at at the Guthman Musical Instrument Competition in 2021!

To read more about it you can on the BELA website

I'm very inspired by microtonal music and all the colors it can bring to music. So I decided to create hardware and software microtonal synthesizers. Then I thought that the software interfaces usually don't reflect dynamics and beauty of the sound and created the 3D visual prototype for the interface in Touch Designer.
